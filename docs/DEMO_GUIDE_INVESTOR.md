## Demo Walkthrough

### Step 1: Login

1. Navigate to https://dev.d2jmg9jtl8alxe.amplifyapp.com/login
2. Enter your credentials
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You will be redirected to your Investor Dashboard

### Step 2: View Available Opportunities

1. In the left sidebar, click **"Available Opportunities"** under Investment Opportunities
2. You will see a $10M Fixed Deposit opportunity from Diamond Trust Bank:
   - **Amount:** $10,000,000 USD
   - **Interest Rate:** 15% APR
   - **Maturity:** 12 months
   - **Expected Return:** $11,500,000 USD
   - **Hedged FX Rate:** 156.57 USD/KES (2% hedging fee)

### Step 3: Accept the Investment Offer

1. Click the **"Accept Offer"** button on the opportunity card
2. Review the investment terms in the modal:
   - Principal amount
   - Expected returns
   - FX terms (spot rate vs hedged rate)
3. Click **"Accept Offer"** to proceed

### Step 4: Upload KYC Documents

You will be asked to upload three documents:

| Document | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Certificate of Incorporation | Your company's incorporation certificate |
| CR12 / International Equivalent | Company registry extract showing directors |
| Proof of Address | Utility bill or bank statement (< 3 months old) |

### Step 5: Provide Bank Account Details

You will need to provide two sets of bank details:

**Source Account (for MT103 pull):**
- Bank Name
- Account Number / IBAN
- SWIFT/BIC Code

**Redemption Account (for maturity payout):**
- Bank Name
- Account Number / IBAN
- SWIFT/BIC Code

### Step 6: Review & Confirm

1. Review all transaction details:
   - Transaction Reference (e.g., TXN-2024-00001)
   - Investment amount and expected return
   - Counterparty (Diamond Trust Bank)
   - KYC status (verified)
   - Source account details

3. Click **"Confirm Investment"**

### Step 7: Transaction Processing

The system will simulate:
- MT103 wire transfer initiation
- Compliance verification
- Settlement confirmation

This takes approximately 2-3 seconds in the demo.

### Step 8: Investment Confirmed!

Upon success, you will see:
- Transaction Reference number
- Amount invested
- Expected return at maturity
- Maturity date
- Option to download your **Transaction Terms Certificate**

### Step 9: View Your Investments

1. Click **"View Investments"** or navigate to **"My Investments"** in the sidebar
2. See your portfolio summary:
   - Total invested
   - Expected returns
   - Total interest earned
   - Active investments count
3. Each investment shows:
   - Progress bar to maturity
   - Days remaining
