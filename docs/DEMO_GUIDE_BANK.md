## Demo Walkthrough

### Step 1: Login

1. Navigate to https://dev.d2jmg9jtl8alxe.amplifyapp.com/login
2. Enter your credentials
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You will be redirected to your Bank Dashboard

### Step 2: Navigate to Capital Calls

1. In the left sidebar, click **"Capital Calls"** under Capital Markets
2. You will see your Capital Calls dashboard with:
   - Total calls created
   - Active calls in progress
   - Completed transactions
   - Total capital raised

### Step 3: Create a New Capital Call

1. Click the **"Create Capital Call"** button (top right)
2. Fill in the instrument details:

| Field | Suggested Value | Description |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| Investment Amount | $10,000,000 | Principal amount you're seeking |
| Maturity Period | 12 months | Fixed deposit term |
| Interest Rate (APR) | 15.0% | Annual interest you'll pay |
| FX Spread | 1.0% | Interbank + spread |
| Hedging Fee | 2.0% | Cost of FX hedging |

3. Click **"Review Terms"**

### Step 4: Review & Publish

On the review screen, you'll see:

**Summary Card:**
- Investment amount and interest rate
- Maturity period
- Expected return to investor
- Offer expiry (7 days)

**FX Details:**
- Current USD/KES spot rate
- Hedged forward rate (with fee)
- Local currency equivalent

**Compliance Checklist:**
- FATCA compliant structure ✓
- Sanctions screening passed ✓
- Capital adequacy ratio: 18.2% ✓
- Moody's rating: A+ ✓
- FDIC sweep network custodian ✓

Click **"Publish to Investors"** to make the offer live.

### Step 5: Notification Sent

Upon publishing:
- An email notification is automatically sent to qualified investors
- The demo sends to: Mathieu.Fourn@shellfoundation.org
- Investors see the opportunity in their dashboard immediately

### Step 6: Monitor Investor Activity

Return to your Capital Calls dashboard to see:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Published** | Offer is live, awaiting investor acceptance |
| **Accepted** | An investor has accepted, KYC pending |
| **KYC Review** | Investor documents submitted, under review |
| **Processing** | Transaction is being processed |
| **Completed** | Capital received, investment active |

Each status includes a progress tracker showing the transaction flow.

### Step 7: Receive Capital Confirmation

When an investor completes the investment:
1. You receive an in-dashboard notification
2. The status updates to **"Completed"**
3. You see **"Capital Received ✓"** confirmation
4. The capital amount is reflected in your totals

## The Complete Flow (What the Investor Sees)

For context, here's what happens on the investor side:

1. **Receive notification** - Email alert about new opportunity
2. **Review terms** - See all details including FX hedging
3. **Accept offer** - Confirm interest in the investment
4. **Upload KYC** - Provide corporate documents (CoI, CR12, address proof)
5. **Bank details** - Provide source and redemption accounts
6. **Review & confirm** - Final confirmation with all terms
7. **Transaction processed** - MT103 initiated, settlement confirmed
8. **Certificate issued** - PDF transaction terms certificate generated
